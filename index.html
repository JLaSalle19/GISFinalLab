Map 1

The problem associated with this map was to locate the neighborhoods in Philadelphia that had at least one school with 1200 or more students within .1 miles of a Philadelphia bike trail. The tan areas represent all the Philadelphia neighborhoods. The green diamonds depict all the Philadelphia schools. The neighborhoods highlighted in blue are the ones that met this criteria.

Map 2

This is a Dot Density Map depicting the Hispanic Population in each state. The map was created to illustrate which states clearly have the highest Hispanic populations The notable standouts are California, Texas, Florida and New York.

Map 3

This is another map that is aimed to illustrate the Hispanic populations in the United States. This is a Choropleth map depicting which states have the highest and lowest percentages of Hispanic populations.The states in dark blue are the states with the highest percentage of Hispanic populations, while the lighter shades of blue illustrate the states with the lowest percentage of Hispanic residents.
